Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How do I sign up for Median?
To sign up for Median, click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and follow the instructions.
Can I use Median on my mobile device?
Currently, you cannot download Median on smartphones. We are working on porting it to iOS and Android.
Is Median free to use?
Yes, Median is free to use, with a text limit of 10 responses per day. To get infinite texts, you can check out our premium plans.
How can I start a debate on Median?
To start a debate on Median, go to the 'Debate' section and click on the 'Start Debate' button. Fill in the required details and submit your debate topic.
Can I invite my friends to join a debate on Median?
Yes, you can invite your friends to join a debate on Median. Simply share the debate link with them or use the 'Invite Friends' feature within the app.
